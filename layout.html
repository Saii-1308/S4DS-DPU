About Us

Our History

Founded in 2018, the Society for Data Science (S4DS) began as a small group of students passionate about data analytics. What started as informal study sessions quickly grew into a recognized student organization with over 200 active members.

In our first year, we hosted 3 workshops. Today, we organize monthly events, annual hackathons, and collaborate with industry leaders to provide our members with unparalleled learning opportunities.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To create a world where data literacy is accessible to all, and data-driven solutions transform every sector of society.

Mission: We empower students from all disciplines to harness the power of data through education, practical experience, and community building.

Core Values:

  • Inclusive learning environment
  • Practical, hands-on education
  • Collaboration across disciplines
  • Ethical data practices
  • Community impact through data
